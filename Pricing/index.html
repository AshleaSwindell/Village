The Village FAQ For Prospective Tenants

Q: How do I schedule an appointment?
A: You can call the Leasing Office and speak to one of our leasing agents to
schedule an appointment Monday-Friday, 9 am-5 pm. (888) 555-8129 or fill out this form.

Q: How soon should I start looking for an apartment?
A: We get our new apartment listings 60-75 days in advance and we recommend that you begin your
apartment search no more than 60 days before your move-in date to choose from the widest selection,
but we usually have listings that accommodate quick move-ins. Please review the criteria for an accepted application.

Q: How many buildings do you have?
A: The Village owns over 55 buildings throughout St.Louis, MO.

Q: What do the buildings look like?
A: Most of our buildings are historic courtyard walk-ups, many of which were designed by renowned
architects and all of which have updated interior spaces, including kitchens, bathrooms and flooring. We also have a few more modern buildings.

Q: How soon do I have to sign my lease after receiving it?
A: We ask that you sign your lease within 5 days of application approval; failure to do so will
result in forfeit of the $300.00 earnest money and loss of the apartment. You may view a sample
lease prior to putting down your earnest money.

Q: How can I pay my rent?
A: You have three options:
-1) Pay online through the tenant portal.
-2) Leave your check in the secure lockbox outside of our management office.
-3) Mail a check to our management office.

Q: Is there a security deposit?
A: The security deposit for Oak Park, River Forest and Forest Park apartments is equal to 1.5 times
the monthly rent. Chicago apartments require a $300.00 non-refundable move-in fee.

Q: How can I apply for an apartment?
A: You can fill out a paper application in our leasing office located at 35 Chicago Ave. or
complete an online application that a leasing agent will provide for you.

Q: Is there an application fee?
A: There is a $40.00 non-refundable application fee. For in-person payment, cash, checks and credit
cards are accepted. If you would like to take a unit off the market after being
accepted, we ask that you also put down $300.00 in earnest money. This payment will be applied to
your security deposit or move-in fee.

Q: How long does it take to process an application?
A: It takes between 24-48 hours to fully process an application. A leasing agent will contact you
with the results as soon as possible. Failure to include all application information or
cooperation from your verification references in a timely manner may delay the process.

Q: Will my application be approved if I have a good salary, but my credit is bad?
A: We require a minimum credit score of 600 to be considered for an apartment.

Q: Can I have a co-signer on my lease?
A: We do not allow co-signers on any of our leases.

Q: Can I smoke in my apartment?
A: Smoking is not allowed in any of our buildings.

Q: How many people can live in an apartment?
A: The occupancy rate is determined in accordance with village or city regulations, typically two
occupants per one bedroom. Occupancy rates may vary.

Q: If my credit and income do not meet the requirements, can I just pay more up front?
A: We do not accept extra or early payment in lieu of meeting the acceptance requirements.

Q: Do you have short-term leases?
A: We offer only 12-month leases.

Q: Is laundry included?
A: Most of our buildings have coin-operated or mobile phone-operated laundry machines in the
basements. Some apartments include in-unit laundry; please check listings for more details.

Q: Is there parking available?
A: Parking is available at some of our units for an additional monthly fee; check listings for
additional information. Otherwise you will need to contact the village or city for street parking permits.
